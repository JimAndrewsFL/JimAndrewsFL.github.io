Overview

The Health Informatics Research Lab focuses on advancing research in health data science, evidence synthesis, and biomedical information systems. The lab integrates methodological research with applied health questions to improve evidence-based decision making.

Research Areas

  • Systematic review methods
  • Biomedical standards and terminologies
  • Health data analytics
