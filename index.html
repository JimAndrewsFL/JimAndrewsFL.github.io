About

Welcome. I am a Professor at the University of South Florida, School of Information. My research intersects with information science and health informatics, and I collaborate with a range of faculty and other partners. Recently, we have been developing a student health informatics research lab with students from different programs across USF. We are continually seeking new collaborators (especially faculty mentors) to develop new research projects. I encourage you to reach out if you have share interests or would have ideas for collaborating.

Research Interests

  • Health informatics
  • Health terminologies and standards
  • Semantic Interoperability
  • Health Information Behaviors
  • Library and Information Science
  • Scholarly Communication and Bibliometrics
